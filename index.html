Professional Profile

I started playing "Valorant" in 2020, starting in the Dominican "Valorant" competition and thanks to my talent, I got the opportunity to participate and have experience in international tournaments and leagues from Latin America.

Thanks to my performance in these leagues, I got the opportunity to become one of the first Dominicans to obtain an E-sports scholarship in the United States at the University of Oklahoma Wesleyan, where I am currently studying and competing for the Valorant team of said university.

I have participated in some of the best teams in Latin America recognized within Valorant and other games, supported and recognized by the ICTL and CvC communities, in which they are the main Valorant leagues. On the other hand, I have also played in other important leagues such as the Aorus League.

Captain of the best Dominican Valorant team for national leagues.
